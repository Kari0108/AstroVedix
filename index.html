Frequently Asked Questions

Building your trust and comfort is our priority. Here, we answer some common questions to help you understand our services better and prepare for your session.

What details do I need to provide for a consultation?

Please share your full birth details (date, time, and place of birth) for astrological sessions. For Tarot or Chakra readings, simply bring your questions or focus areas.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

While having your exact birth time helps provide the most accurate astrological insights, don't worry if you don't know it. We can still work with an approximate time or use other techniques to help narrow it down during the consultation. Please share as much information as you have, and we'll guide you accordingly.

Can I ask follow-up questions after my reading?

Absolutely! We encourage ongoing dialogue. You can reach out via email or WhatsApp for clarifications or additional guidance after your session.

Are sessions conducted online or in-person?

Currently, all sessions are held online through Zoom, Google Meet, or WhatsApp Video to offer you convenience and accessibility, no matter where you are.

How do I prepare for my session?

Find a quiet space, have your questions ready, and keep an open mind. For astrology, ensure you have accurate birth details handy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via UPI and NEFT/IMPS bank transfers. Payment can be made during the booking process or as mutually agreed.
